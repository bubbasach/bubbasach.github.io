3 months together.

I had no idea life could ever feel this way. I wake up with new life everyday that you are by my side. Whether we spend the day rotting with familia moderna, or we spend the day rotting with cat reels, I can count on you being next to me, be it rain shine wind sleet snow.

I’ve been thinking a lot about our banter, and how it’s changed into babying. I know that we’ve both reflected on that and wondered where our zip went. And what matters isn’t who we are, or what we act like. But who we’re with. And as long as I’m with you, and we are together on this journey that is life, the details don’t bother me.

You are my forever gal. My one and only. My gorgeous girl.

I love you forever Meera.
